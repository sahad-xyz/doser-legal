Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a session?

From the Home tab, tap the "Quick Log" button. Select an experience, enter the date and duration, and tap Save. You can also use the built-in timer to track sessions in real time.

Can I import hours I've already tracked?

Yes. From the Home tab, tap the import button to bulk import past sessions. You can add multiple sessions at once with different dates and durations.

What categories can I track?

doser supports Clinical, Shadowing, Research, Volunteering, Leadership, Teaching, Employment, Extracurricular, and Other.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. All data is securely synced to the cloud. If you switch devices, just sign in with the same account and everything will be there.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data and cannot be undone.

I found a bug or have a feature request.
